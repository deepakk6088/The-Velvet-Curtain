Our Story

Five Decades of Theatrical Excellence

Since 1974, The Velvet Curtain has been the heart of our community's cultural life. Our historic 800-seat theater has hosted everything from intimate dramas to spectacular musicals, always with the same commitment to artistic excellence and audience experience.

Our resident company of talented actors, directors, and designers work together to create productions that move, inspire, and entertain audiences of all ages.
